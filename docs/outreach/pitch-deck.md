# ComfySeniors — Facility Pitch Deck

**Purpose:** A 12-slide deck used for facility outreach calls, partnership conversations, and investor updates. Written in markdown so it can be pasted into Gamma.app, Pitch, Beautiful.ai, or Canva to generate slides in ~2 minutes.

**Recommended tool:** [Gamma.app](https://gamma.app) — paste this whole file and it auto-generates a branded deck with the ComfySeniors brand colors (cs-blue: #1E3A8A, cs-lavender: #A78BFA, off-white background).

**Length:** 12 slides. Total read time ≈ 4 minutes.

## Slide 1 — Title

### ComfySeniors

## Slide 2 — The Problem (for families)

### Senior care is the biggest financial and emotional decision most families ever make — and the information to make it well is hidden.

- Prices: not published anywhere
- Inspection records: buried on CMS.gov, 4 clicks deep, in bureaucratic language
- Staff turnover: not visible on any consumer-facing site
- Reviews: gamed by "luxury" branding and paid placement services

**Families are choosing billion-dollar facilities with less data than they have when buying a used car.**

---

## Slide 3 — The Problem (for facilities)

### Facilities get buried under placement-service middlemen that charge $5,000-$8,000 per move-in and give the family nothing verifiable.

The status quo for facilities:
- Pay placement services one month's rent per resident
- No control over how your facility is described
- Competing against 50+ facilities on identical directory layouts
- Inspection data is either hidden or weaponized against you

**Facilities with good citation histories have no way to prove it. Facilities with bad ones can hide it behind glossy photos.**

---

## Slide 4 — Our Position

### We show everything. Then we let families decide.

Every facility page on ComfySeniors shows:
- **Real pricing** (range, benchmark vs. county avg)
- **Full inspection history** from CMS + state licensing agencies
- **Every citation**, categorized by severity, with plain-English summaries
- **Staff turnover** (RN + total, color-coded vs. state avg)
- **Value score** (0-100, quantifying price vs. quality)
- **Verified badge** for facilities that claim and maintain their listing

Families come for data. They come back because they trust us.

## Slide 8 — What We Don't Do

### Transparency works both ways.

- We **do not** sell family contact information to anyone — ever.
- We **do not** let facilities pay to hide reviews.
- We **do not** let facilities pay to hide inspection data.
- We **do not** rank facilities by how much they pay us.
- We **do not** spam families after they inquire.

The Verified plan buys you presentation tools and cost structure — not editorial control.

## Appendix: Speaker Notes

### Slide 2 — The Problem (for families)
Open with a story if speaking live: "A family I spoke to last month spent three weekends touring facilities before realizing the one they loved had been cited for medication errors in every inspection since 2019. The data existed — it was just three clicks deep on a government website."

### Slide 6 — The Model
If the prospect pushes back on $297/mo: remind them it's $9.77/day. Less than the coffee run most administrators make. And it eliminates a $5K-$8K variable cost.

### Slide 7 — Why Verified Wins
Numbers are averages; ask the prospect how many move-ins they typically do per year. Do the math out loud with their number. If they do even ONE placement per year through a directory, Verified is cheaper than one placement fee.

### Slide 8 — What We Don't Do
This slide is the reason facilities trust us. Don't skip it. The "no" list is more persuasive than the "yes" list.

### Slide 11 — The Ask
If they hesitate, lead them to the free claim. "You don't need to decide about Verified today. Just claim the listing so at least you control what families see about you." Low-friction entry converts best.
